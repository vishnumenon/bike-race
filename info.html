Race Info

The Duke Multiple Myeloma Bike Ride is a 25K or 50 K or 100 K road bike ride from the Duke campus through rural Durham and Orange Counties. Your registration fee will help support the families and patients undergoing treatment at the Multiple Myeloma Center at Duke as well as to support research against this disease. These three rides will start in front of Duke Hospital North Pavilion 2400 Pratt Street, Durham 27705 and tour through rural Durham and Orange Counties before returning to Duke. The terrain is typical for the Piedmont area of North Carolina. The roads wind through forests and farms with short but frequent rolling hills. The 100 K route continues on into rural Orange County before returning to Duke. The 25K, 50K and 100K rides will begin at 8am. All three routes will stay open until 3 pm. There will be rest stops and SAG vans throughout the routes.
